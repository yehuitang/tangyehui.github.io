Yehui Tang (唐业辉)

I am a 4th-year PhD student at school of artifical intelligence, Peking University, supervised by Prof. Chao Xu. Currently, I am working as a research intern at Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab, mentored by Dr. Yunhe Wang. I obtained B.S. from Xidian University at 2018. My main research interest lies in base models for computer vision (e.g., CNN, Vision Transformer, Vision MLP) and model compression (e.g, pruning).
